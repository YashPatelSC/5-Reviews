Hotel Policies & Guest Notes

Pet Policy:
Pets are welcome at our property. A fee of $25 + tax per pet, per night applies. Each pet must not exceed 75 lbs. A maximum of two (2) pets is allowed per room. Service animals are permitted without charge in accordance with applicable laws.

Incidental Deposit Policy:
A refundable incidental deposit of $50 is required at check-in. This deposit may be used to cover damages, missing items, or policy violations and will be refunded after checkout if no issues are found. Reservations booked using Choice Privileges points require a $250 refundable deposit.

Smoking Policy:
This is a non-smoking property. Smoking in a non-smoking room will result in a $150 cleaning penalty charged to the guest account.

Early Check-In & Late Check-Out:
Early check-in and late check-out requests are subject to availability and are not guaranteed. Please contact the front desk for availability and approval. Additional fees may apply depending on timing, occupancy, and room availability.
